## 📐 **Room Types & Configurations**

### **Standard Room Types**
- **Bedrooms**: Standard (10'x10'), Master (12'x14'), Guest (10'x10')
- **Living Areas**: Living rooms (12'x14'), Family rooms, TV lounges
- **Dining**: Formal dining (10'x10'), Breakfast nooks, Open dining
- **Kitchen**: Modular kitchen (8'x10'), Open kitchen, Utility areas
- **Bathrooms**: Master bath, Common bath, Guest bath (5'x7')
- **Special Rooms**: Study (8'x10'), Home office, Prayer room, Store

### **Additional Features**
- **Balconies**: Front/back balconies (4'x6' minimum)
- **Terraces**: Rooftop or ground-level terraces
- **Parking**: Covered parking (8'x16' per car)
- **Staircases**: Straight, L-shaped, spiral designs
- **Setbacks**: Automatic application based on plot boundaries

## 🏗️ **Plot Sizes & Constraints**

### **Supported Plot Sizes**
- **Small Plots**: 20'x30' to 30'x40' (Compact designs)
- **Medium Plots**: 30'x40' to 40'x60' (Standard homes)
- **Large Plots**: 40'x60' to 60'x80' (Luxury layouts)
- **Custom Sizes**: Any rectangular plot dimensions

### **Facing Options**
- **North Facing**: Optimal for natural light
- **East Facing**: Morning sun benefits
- **South Facing**: Afternoon light optimization
- **West Facing**: Evening light considerations

### **Setback Requirements**
- **Front Setback**: 6-10 feet (municipal requirements)
- **Side Setbacks**: 4-6 feet each side
- **Rear Setback**: 4-8 feet
- **Corner Plots**: Special setback rules
